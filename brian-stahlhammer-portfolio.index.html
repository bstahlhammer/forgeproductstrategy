About Brian

Strategy forged from
three decades of doing.

Brian Stahlhammer is a senior product leader who has spent 20+ years at the intersection of technology, strategy, and transformation — turning ambitious ideas into market-defining products.

His career began with a mechanical engineering degree from Colorado State and early consulting experience at PwC and Accenture, where he honed the analytical rigor that would define everything that followed. A Georgetown MBA sharpened his strategic lens.

Operational roles at Gogo, GCI, CableLabs, Calix, and FreeWave gave him the battle scars that only come from execution. Most recently at FreeWave Technologies, Brian led the transformation of a 30-year-old hardware company into a cybersecurity Platform-as-a-Service business — establishing category leadership in Operational Zero Trust security and driving significant profitability improvements.

Today, Brian channels that hard-won expertise into Forge Product Strategy — advising CEOs and CPOs of mid-sized companies navigating their own product strategy pivots and go-to-market transformations.

The best product strategies aren't found in frameworks — they're forged at the intersection of deep customer empathy, rigorous market analysis, and the courage to make hard tradeoffs.

— Brian Stahlhammer
