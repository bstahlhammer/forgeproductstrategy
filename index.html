About Brian

Strategy that comes from
actually doing the work.

I started my career as a mechanical engineer at Colorado State, then spent years at PwC and Accenture doing management consulting. That combination of technical depth and business rigor is still the foundation of how I think about product.

After getting my MBA at Georgetown, I moved into operational product roles where things got real fast. At Gogo, GCI, CableLabs, Calix, and FreeWave, I wasn't advising from the outside. I was in the room, making the tradeoffs, building the teams, and owning the outcomes.

The work I'm most proud of at FreeWave was turning a 30-year-old hardware company into a cybersecurity platform business. That kind of transformation, where you're rebuilding the revenue model, the go-to-market motion, and the product architecture all at once, is exactly what Forge is built to help other companies navigate.

I've worked across four continents and managed international teams in India, China, Brazil, and Vietnam. I've built paywalls, billing systems, and licensing infrastructure from scratch. And I've seen what separates the companies that successfully pivot from the ones that don't.

"The best product strategies aren't found in frameworks. They're forged at the intersection of deep customer empathy, rigorous market analysis, and the courage to make hard tradeoffs."
Brian Stahlhammer, Founder, Forge Product Strategy

01
FreeWave Technologies · Boulder, CO · Cybersecurity
Turning a 30-year hardware company into a cybersecurity platform

FreeWave had built a strong reputation in industrial wireless over three decades, but the market was shifting toward software and recurring revenue. I led the full product strategy transformation, defining Operational Zero Trust as a category, rebuilding the go-to-market motion for enterprise sales, and architecting the recurring revenue infrastructure that made the pivot financially viable.

Calix · Telecommunications · Community Wi-Fi
Scaling SmartTown Wi-Fi to 10 million device connections

At Calix I took their SmartTown platform from early traction to 10M+ connected devices, while maintaining high NPS scores across the subscriber base.
